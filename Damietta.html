ITA-SDGs

6th Olympiad Participant

I collaborated with a team under the guidance of Dr. Mahmoud Y. Shams to participate in the 6th IT Olympiad for the Computer Science and Informatics Sector (ITA-SDGs). Our project aimed to address challenges in traditional classrooms by developing a smart classroom assistant, integrating models for gaze direction analysis to assess focus levels and face recognition technology for automatic attendance. The project received notable recognition and was featured in Youm7 - اليوم السابع.
